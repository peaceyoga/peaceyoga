ॐ Peace Yoga ॐ

Hello, my name is Kusum Hooda, and I am a passionate yoga teacher with a profound dedication to nurturing holistic well-being. Over the years, I have immersed myself in the transformative world of yoga, blending ancient wisdom with modern insights to guide my students on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. My classes are a harmonious fusion of dynamic asanas, mindful breathwork, and enriching meditation, creating a space where individuals can flourish both physically and mentally. What sets me apart is not only my technical expertise but also my commitment to fostering a sense of community and belonging. Every student in my class is valued and supported as they progress toward wellness. I believe in embodying serenity and authenticity, and my aim is to inspire others to embrace balance and live purposefully. I am excited to bring my passion and dedication to you and continue spreading the transformative benefits of yoga.
